Meet Dr. Sunny Priyatham

Dr. Sunny Priyatham is a dedicated dental professional with over 13 years of experience in general dentistry and 8 years of specialized experience in pediatric dentistry. He completed his Bachelor of Dental Surgery (BDS) from Army College of Dental Sciences in 2012. He further pursued his Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) in Pedodontics from Narayana Dental College and Hospital under Dr. NTR University of Health Sciences, graduating in 2017. Dr. Sunny began his professional journey as a GDMO (Dental Officer) at CHC Diglipur in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands under the Union Territory Health Mission (2012-2013), where he gained valuable experience in community dental care. He has held key academic positions, including: Assistant Professor, Department of Pedodontics at Malla Reddy Dental College and Hospital (2018-2020) Assistant Professor, Department of Pedodontics at Dr. D. Y. Patil Dental College and Hospital (2021-2024) Currently, he serves as an Associate Professor in the Department of Pedodontics at Saveetha Dental College and Hospital, part of Saveetha Institute of Medical and Technical Sciences.
